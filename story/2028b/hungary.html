Hungary - June

When Hungary sealed its borders, refusing to export what little grain it had left, Serbia retaliated. At first, it was just skirmishes along the border. Then, it turned into a full-scale conflict. By the time NATO called for de-escalation, Serbian forces had already crossed into Hungarian farmland, looting grain silos and setting fire to storage depots. Hungary responded with airstrikes. And suddenly, Europe had its first war over food.
