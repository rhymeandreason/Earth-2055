Earth 2055.

We have done the right things to create a more abundant and balanced future.

We didn't always think we would get there. A lot happens in 30 years.

A catastrophic agricultural collapse unfolds.

2028

Suddenly, nature raised the stakes...promises were no longer enough.

Widespread droughts, heatwaves, and floods hammer farming regions.

An aggressive pathogen decimates staple crops — rice, wheat, corn, soybeans.

2028 Summer

The world spiraled into chaos.

By late summer, humanity was staring into the abyss. The food crisis was no longer just a disaster—it was a reckoning.

A reckoning for decades of unsustainable farming, climate inaction, and blind faith in the invincibility of modern supply chains.

2028 Fall

Yet human resilience was faster than politics.

Across the globe, individuals and communities took matters into their own hands, determined to survive.

Food became bigger than money or ideology.
